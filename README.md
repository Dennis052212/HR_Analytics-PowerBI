\# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)



\## 🔍 Overview



This project is an end-to-end \*\*HR Analytics Dashboard\*\* built in Power BI, designed to deliver actionable insights into workforce trends such as \*\*attrition, hiring, recruitment efficiency, and compensation\*\*.



The dashboard follows a \*\*business-focused approach aligned with U.S. HR analytics practices\*\*, making it suitable for enterprise environments and remote data roles.



\---



\## 🎯 Objectives



\* Analyze \*\*employee attrition and retention trends\*\*

\* Track \*\*hiring performance and workforce growth\*\*

\* Evaluate \*\*salary distribution and pay equity\*\*

\* Measure \*\*recruitment efficiency and hiring costs\*\*

\* Identify \*\*high-risk departments and employee segments\*\*



\---



\## 📁 Dataset



The project includes multiple integrated datasets:



\### 🔹 HR Dataset (`hr\_dataset`)



\* Employee demographics, salary, tenure, and attrition

\* Core workforce analytics foundation



\### 🔹 Recruitment Dataset (`recruitment\_dataset`)



\* Candidate pipeline and recruitment sources

\* Recruiter performance tracking

\* Funnel analysis and hiring conversion metrics



\### 🔹 Hiring Dataset (`hiring\_dataset`)



\* Hiring records and workforce growth trends

\* Hiring cost breakdown and recruitment expenses



\### 🔹 Supporting Dimension Tables



\* Department

\* Job Role

\* Location

\* Education

\* Recruitment Source

\---



\## 📈 Key Insights



\* Employees with \*\*less than 2 years tenure\*\* show higher attrition, indicating onboarding gaps

\* Customer-facing roles experience higher turnover, suggesting workload or engagement challenges

\* Mid-salary range (\*\*$80K–$100K\*\*) shows increased attrition, highlighting career mobility trends

\* Recruitment funnel analysis reveals opportunities to improve \*\*conversion rates from interview to hire\*\*

\* Minimal gender pay gap suggests relatively balanced compensation practices
